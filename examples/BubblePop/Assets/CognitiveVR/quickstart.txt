cognitiveVR plugin for Unity - v0.2


Quickstart:
1. Sign up at http://dashboard.cognitivevr.io

2. Login and retrieve your cogntiveVR Customer ID. See https://github.com/cognitiveVR/cvr-sdk-unity for details
about where to find your cognitiveVR Customer ID

3. Select your VR Unity Plugin from the CognitiveVR Settings Popup

Add CognitiveVR/_Sample/CognitiveVR_BasicInit.prefab to your scene if you have not added it from the popup

That's it! You are now tracking your user's basic data including GPU,CPU,OS,RAM. If you are using the SteamVR plugin,
you will also recieve the current room size
